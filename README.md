# HonestBill

**Flat-rate time tracking & invoicing for freelancers and small agencies — that can't surprise-bill you.**

Got burned when your billing tool got acquired and repriced overnight? HonestBill is the flat-rate, founder-owned alternative with its pricing promise *in writing* — so that can't happen to you here.

## The promise — our Pricing Charter

- 💸 **No usage billing. Ever.** One flat price per workspace — never per invoice, client, project, or task.
- 🔒 **Capped, predictable pricing.** No overnight 10× renewal shocks, and 90 days' notice on any change.
- 📤 **Your data is always free to leave with.** One-click export, no lock-in, no ransom.
- 🧩 **Open & self-hostable core.** If we ever broke a promise, you could run it yourself.
- 🛡️ **Acquisition-proof.** The charter transfers with the company.

## What it does

- ⏱️ **Track time** — a live timer or manual entry, per client & project
- 🧾 **Invoice in a click** — turn unbilled time & expenses into a tax-ready invoice
- 📥 **Switch in minutes** — one-click **import from Harvest** (CSV or API) so you keep your history
- 📊 **See where you stand** — unbilled work, outstanding, and paid-this-month at a glance
- 💰 **Get paid** *(coming soon)* — send invoices with a pay-by-card link

## Who it's for

Solo freelancers and small (2–15 person) creative, dev, and consulting agencies who bill by time and are tired of tools that reprice them once they're locked in.
